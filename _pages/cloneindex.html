Our Grandview, WA facility is fully operational, and we are well underway with the 2018 growing season. We are now producing concentrate from our flash frozen 2017 crop of 7,000 pounds of cleaned and cured cannabis that has been in cryostorage. This provides the licensed entities an ample supply of high-quality raw product to successfully execute our 2018 market launch and achieve our revenue generation targets. Our experience in 2017 has taught us a considerable amount that is already increasing our efficiencies for the 2018 season in Washington. We have optimized our cultivation methodologies and significantly reduced our pesticide use while maintaining plant health and yield, which sets a solid foundation for future grow operations in other states. We maintain our view that raw cannabis prices will continue to decline; the key to maintaining profit margins is continual innovation in efficient growing.
