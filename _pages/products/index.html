With market pricing for extracted oils and concentrates remaining strong in demand and price while the market for canna flower continues its commoditization, we have established a strong set of product offers to take advantage of these economic factors. In addition to our branded cannabis extraction array of products, the company will be entering these 2 very interesting and growing wholesale markets. We will be offering a base cannabis oil to be used in the production of edibles and canna vapor products.

We are embarking on a 3 channel strategy to maximize the monetization of everything we grow into oil;

  • Branded-CO2 BHO extracts and distillates in 3 product lines
  • Private Label-bulk oil supply to large regional/national brands
  • Food Service-base oils to companies focused on creating edibles
