With market pricing for extracted oils and concentrates remaining strong in demand and price while the market for canna flower continues its commoditization, the licensed entities have established a strong set of product offerings to take advantage of these economic factors. The licensed entities will be offering a base cannabis oil to be used in the production of edibles and canna vapor products.

The licensed entities are embarking on a 3-channel strategy to maximize the monetization of everything that is grown into oil;

  • Branded-CO2 BHO extracts and distillates in 3 product lines
  • Private Label-bulk oil supply to large regional/national brands
  • Food Service-base oils to companies focused on creating edibles
