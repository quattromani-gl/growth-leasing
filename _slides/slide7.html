Refinition Advisors builds and curates audiences for your and your competitors physical locations with the accuracy and immediacy previously known only to online content or e-tailers. The creation of data-rich custom digital audiences based on actual visitors to physical locations unlocks an incredibly valuable opportunity for marketers to better learn about their audiences and their competitors, to spend their marketing dollars far more wisely, achieve better results, and to sustainably build their businesses without costly marketing guesswork.
